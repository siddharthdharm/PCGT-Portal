Mytrah Energy (India) Pvt. Ltd.

Headquartered in London, UK, the Mytrah Group is focused on delivering sustainable energy in a world without subsidies. Mytrah Energy (India) Pvt. Ltd. aims to own and operate 3500 MW of Renewable Power in India. Currently, it has a total wind portfolio energy capacity of 1000 MW. These assets are spread across fifteen wind farms in eight states - Rajasthan, Gujarat, Madhya Pradesh, Maharashtra, Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, Karnataka and Tamil Nadu. The company's portfolio was built using a combination of 'turn-key' developers and in-house project development, with wind turbines purchased from leading international vendors.

For further info, one can visit their website - Mytrah Energy (India) Pvt. Ltd.

Collaboration with Birla Institute of Technology and Science (BITS), Pilani

Mytrah Energy (India) Pvt. Ltd. has signed an MoU with BITS Pilani Hyderabad Campus in order to hire college students (as interns) and give them projects which will be useful to the company, such as creating Automation Tools and Softwares. This, in turn, helps the students getting exposed to the "corporate world". Difficulties faced in real-life problems are brought to light. Apart from that, students learn to apply their educational knowledge and come up with different approaches to solve a given problem.

Birla Institute of Technology and Science (BITS), Pilani - Hyderabad

Established in 2008, BITS Pilani Hyderabad Campus is the fourth BITS campus after Pilani, Dubai and Goa. It serves as a home to around 4000 undergraduates, 500 M.Tech students, and 100 Ph.D scholars. Around 5 lakh students write the BITSAT (Entrance Exam for Engineering) every year, and only 2200 students are selected (across all the campuses). Being the best private institution in the country, BITS Pilani admits students based only on pure merit.
One can get more information on the BITS Pilani website.
